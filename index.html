Welcome to Chepalungu Technical Training Institute

Chepalungu Technical Training Institute (CHETTI) is a Public TVET Institution opening its doors to the public for full and part-time certified academic programmes. CHETTI is positioning itself as a key source of highly skilled and competent labour force, and reputable entrepreneurs in accordance with the needs and specifications of the ever evolving world industry, for the realiztion of Vision 2030. Our service delivery is unrivaled and is premised on best practices, and there is no doubting that CHETTI is a hotbed of innovations and solutions. Our trainees will enjoy a continuous on and off-campus academic experience from the finest, well refined, top notched, tested and proven trianers .For more information about our courses, download our prospectus here!.

Diploma Courses

We offer various kinds of Diploma Courses: from Graduate to Postgraduate and Advanced Diploma. We offer Diploma Courses in a wide variety of subjects including Arts, Humanities, Business, Biological and Engineering, Technology, and more.

Certificate Courses

We offer the best Certificate Courses in Agriculture, Businness, Engineering, and Technology according to the evolving needs and the demands of the industrial world.

Craft Courses

We offer Craft Courses that empower young people with technical skills that make them more employable or innovative to meet the standars of the job market or launch their own businesses.
